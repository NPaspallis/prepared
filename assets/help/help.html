The PREPARED Educational App

This app aims to provide engaging, concise content for optimal training and impact.

The content consists of interactive case studies and short ethics modules.

Each case study covers a topic which presents different perspectives of problems that encompass ethical dilemmas.

Learners interact by means of small steps which present data as text, images, videos, dialogues, and polls.

For feature requests and bug reports, please refer to the project webpage open_in_new.

Some of the clipart used in the case studies is based on content from the publicdomainvectors.org open_in_new.
